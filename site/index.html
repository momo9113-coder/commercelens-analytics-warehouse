Operational findings

  • Delivery and reviews: among reviewed eligible orders, late deliveries averaged 2.567 stars versus 4.294 on time, a 1.728-point gap. The 1-2 star review rate was 44.8 percentage points higher in the late group.
  • Monthly reliability: March 2018 had the highest late-delivery rate among months with at least 1,000 eligible orders: 21.4% across 7,003 orders.
  • Geography: Among states with at least 1,000 eligible orders, CE had a 15.3% late rate versus 5.0% in PR, a descriptive gap of 10.3%.
  • Seller dispersion: Across 210 sellers with at least 100 eligible orders, the median late rate was 6.7%, the 90th percentile was 12.8%, and the seller-level late-rate/review correlation was -0.466.

Method and reproduction

The late-delivery denominator includes only orders with both actual and estimated delivery timestamps. SQL definitions are versioned in analysis.sql; interpretation and limitations are documented in docs/ANALYSIS.md.

python -m commercelens.cli report --data-dir data/raw --db-path reports/commercelens.duckdb --output-dir site
